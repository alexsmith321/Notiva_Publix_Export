Privacy Policy for Notiva

Last updated: November 2025

Notiva does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The application runs entirely offline on your device and does not use any external servers.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information
  • No microphone recordings
  • No analytics
  • No tracking data
  • No location data

Microphone Access

Notiva requests access to the microphone solely to detect pitch in real time for sight-reading exercises. No audio is recorded, stored, uploaded, or transmitted.

Children’s Privacy

Notiva collects no data and stores nothing. It is safe for use by children and compliant with COPPA and GDPR regulations.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include advertisements, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking libraries.
